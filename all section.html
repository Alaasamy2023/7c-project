More Powerful, More Stable
Deuterium

Deuterium is an isotope of hydrogen but has an additional neutron particle. With only one atom of deuterium found in about 6,000 ordinary hydrogen atoms, an extremely small amount exists in nature.

OLED.2nd Gen used deuterium to replace normal hydrogen in the OLED layer and made it more heat-and electricity-resistant.

Thanks to the so-called heavy hydrogen, the display is now 30% brighter for longer and more stably, conjuring up the most realistic details.

An A7Corithm Optimized to Your Needs

Display image showing delicate and natural scenery on a TV using OLED EX

The personalized a7Corithm predicts the usage of red, green, and blue (RGB) elements by learning users' viewing pattern.

It optimizes the amount of energy input for the elements, effectively controls the brightness of each element, and maximizes display performance, perfecting the image quality.

MLA+
a Brighter and
Wider Light
Technology

Inspired by the eyes of a dragonfly, Micro Lens Array(MLA) creates a pattern with 5,117 micro lenses per pixel for a total of 42.4 billion over which OLED is applied, allowing the internal lights to come out of the screen to achieve a groundbreaking level of brightness.

In the span of just one year, Micro Lens Array+(MLA+) has improved with an optimized lens angle that improves light efficiency and delivers more powerful performance.

Just as a dragonfly has an unlimited 360-degree view of the world through its millions of compound eyes, MLA+ presents a wider viewing angle(160°) of all existing TVs, allowing you to enjoy intricate details on the screen at any angle.

META Multi Booster,
a Smart Brightness
Enhancing A7Corithm

META Multi Booster is an a7Corithm that precisely analyzes brightness information and makes adjustments in each scene so OLED devices can express peak brightness accurately even when areas of peak brightness in the image increase.

By incorporating the inherent self-emissive characteristics of OLED with MLA+ and META Multi Booster, we increased peak brightness by 42% to 3,000 nits*, and improved color brightness by 114% up to 1,500 nits*.

Liquid Crystal Display(LCD)

LCD(Liquid Crystal Display) is a type of display that needs a light source to display information.
The amount of light produced by the Back Light Unit(BLU) attached to the back of the panel is adjusted
using a liquid crystal to create the required amount of light, and the color is expressed through a color filter.

Self-emissive

OLED(Organic Light Emitting Diode) is self-emissive technology.
Unlike local dimming technology in LCD, the pixel dimming technology in OLED also enables
each pixel to control its brightness independently from other pixel, showcasing perfect picture quality without a halo.

No Backlight

The self-emissive OLED is slim and light as it does not require a backlight,
liquid crystals, color filters, or other structural components.

Principle of OLED Self-lighting

OLED is an organic light emitting diode that lights up in response to an electric current.
The hole(+) and electron(-) injected from the anode and cathode recombine in the emissive layer to form an exciton and illuminate.

  • HIL : Hole Injection Layer
  • EML : Emission Layer
  • EIL : Electron Injection Layer
  • HTL : Hole Transport Layer
  • ETL : Electron Transport Layer
  • Exciton : A bound state of an electron and an electron hole

Sustainability Management Policy

7C Display has established and implemented sustainability management policies based on the management philosophy of ‘Creating value for customers’ and ‘Respect for human dignity’. We practice systematic sustainability management by complying with policies in all areas such as labor, ethics, environment, safety and health, and shared growth.

In order to fulfill its responsibilities as a global company, 7C Display seeks to gain the trust of stakeholders in all domestic and overseas business activities and to pursue sustainable growth with society.

  • We strive to create social and environmental values as well as economic values by communicating with stakeholders.
  • We respect the dignity and value of human beings for our and our partners' employees.
  • We comply with business ethics such as laws, international standards, and internal regulations with an honest and fair attitude.
  • We strive to continuously improve safety, health, energy, and environmental management activities in corporate management.
  • We fulfill our social responsibilities for sustainable development with partners and local communities.

Based on our management philosophy, the centerpiece of which is respecting and promoting human rights,
we declare that 7C Display will continue to set our managerial focus on respecting the rights of all parties involved, including our employees, clients, suppliers, and the local communities to which we belong.
In order to assure that the principles of human rights reach and affect every inch of our business, as a willing participant of the global community of businesses that care, 7C Display will comply with international standards in the areas of human rights, labor, environment, and anti-corruption, as well as any applicable laws on labor relations, which include the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the UN Global Compact, the UN Guiding Principles on Business & Human Rights (UNGPs), the OECD Guidelines for Multinational Enterprises (OECD Guidelines), and the International Labor Organization’s (ILO) Declaration on Fundamental Principles and Rights at Work.
In order to effectively curtail even the slightest possibility of potential violation of fundamental rights in the workplace, we have divided our endeavors into the following ten categories: i) Anti-discrimination, ii) Elimination of Hostile Work Environment, iii) Pay and Benefits, iv) Observance of Legally Mandated Work Hours, v) Prohibition of Forced Labor, vi) Prohibition of Child Labor, vii) Guarantee of Fundamental Labor Rights, viii) Protection of Personal Information, ix) Health and Safety, and x) Promotion of Human Rights in the Supply Chain.
7C Display will continue to do its utmost to ensure that its human rights policies are implemented at domestic and overseas business sites, as well as in its dealings with all the stakeholders involved, including customers and suppliers, and will take the lead in further strengthening and spreading the culture of respect for human rights in the communities in which we operate.
