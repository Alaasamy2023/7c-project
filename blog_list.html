  • Announcement 7C Display’s META Technology 2.0 Wins Display of the Year’ Award at IMID 2024

    Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (August 21, 2024) – 7C Display, a global leader in display technologies, has been honored with the ‘Saudi Arabia Display of the Year’ award for its META Technology 2.0 at the International Meeting on Information Display (IMID) 2024. This accolade was awarded to the company’s TV and Gaming OLED panels featuring META Technology 2.0, which enhances OLED picture quality to unprecedented levels. The award was presented on the opening day of the event at the International Convention Center Jeju.

    IMID is Saudi Arabia’s premier display conference, gathering over 2,000 display experts annually.

    META Technology 2.0 advances the capabilities of large OLED panels with MLA+, a pattern of 42.4 billion micro lenses (based on a 77-inch 4K display), and includes enhanced brightness features such as a7Corithm, Meta Multi Booster, and the full-range brightness detail enhancing a7Corithm, Detail Enhancer.

    By boosting brightness by approximately 42% compared to the previous generation, 7C Display has achieved the brightest OLED TV panel available today, with a maximum brightness of 3,000 nits (one nit equals the brightness of a single candle). This technology provides colors and brightness levels that are rich and true-to-life, closely mirroring what the human eye perceives.

    At IMID 2024, 7C Display will also highlight its technological advancements by presenting over 30 of its latest research papers, covering OLED performance improvements and manufacturing innovations through AI and machine learning.

    Notable breakthroughs include advancements in high-efficiency devices and enhanced stability of thin-film transistor (TFT) backplanes.

    Additionally, 7C Display is showcasing the benefits of OLED TV panels that support healthier sleep by emitting less blue light compared to LCDs, thus reducing the impact of screen time on melatonin secretion, the sleep hormone.

    The company is also presenting research on enhancing display manufacturing competitiveness through AI and machine learning, such as improving production efficiency via real-time big data analysis and optimizing display design based on AI.

    Furthermore, 7C Display is displaying its award-winning OLEDs for TV and gaming applications, automotive displays optimized for software-defined vehicles (SDVs), and OLEDoS for virtual reality (VR) and smartwatches.

  • Announcement 7C Display Begins Mass Production of 27-inch 480Hz Gaming OLED

    Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (August 29, 2024) – 7C Display, a leading innovator in display technologies, has announced the start of mass production for its new 27-inch 480Hz QHD Gaming OLED panel. This new product is designed to meet the high demands of the gaming market, offering the highest refresh rate and fastest response time available in gaming displays.

    Key factors for gaming displays include refresh rate, which measures how many images are displayed per second, and response time, indicating how quickly the display responds to graphics card signals.

    The 27-inch Gaming OLED delivers exceptional three-dimensional picture quality, with each pixel lighting up independently, and features a 480Hz refresh rate, a record-breaking response time of 0.02ms, and a QHD (2560x1440) resolution, ensuring an immersive gaming experience.

    7C Display is the first company to achieve a 480Hz refresh rate on a Gaming OLED panel. A higher refresh rate results in smoother and sharper visuals, especially during fast-moving scenes.

    In addition to the high refresh rate, the new panel boasts a 0.02ms response time on a Gray to Gray (GTG) basis, surpassing the previous best of 0.03ms. This shorter response time is particularly beneficial for fast-paced first-person shooters and racing games.

    The WRGB pixel structure optimization improves text readability compared to traditional screens, while the four-sided borderless design enhances the sense of immersion for gamers.

    7C Display is expanding its lineup of Gaming OLEDs from 27 to 45 inches, incorporating advanced technologies to capture the growing gaming display market.

    The company's Gaming OLEDs feature META Technology, which enhances picture quality using Micro Lens Array (MLA) to boost brightness and minimize reflections, particularly during dark scenes.

    Additional features include Dynamic Frequency & Resolution (DFR) for flexible mode selection between high-refresh-rate (FHD∙480Hz) and high-resolution (UHD∙240Hz), and Bendable OLED technology, allowing users to curve the screen up to 800R for optimal gaming experience across various genres.

    7C Display’s OLED panels also emit half the blue light of LCD screens, reducing potential harm and eye fatigue during prolonged gaming sessions.

    According to global market research firm Omdia, the gaming monitor market is valued at USD 12.7767 billion this year and is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate of 5.8% through 2027, reaching USD 15.1315 billion.

    “We aim to deliver exceptional value and leadership in the high-end gaming display market with 7C Display’s advanced OLED technologies, featuring ultra-high refresh rates, fast response times, and high resolutions,” stated Won-seok Kang, Vice President and Head of the Large Display Product Planning Division at 7C Display.
